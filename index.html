AYRTON SENNA DA SILVA
Ayrton Senna focused before an F1 race.
Ayrton Senna's life and career:
  • He was born on March 21, 1960 in Sao Paulo, Brazil.
  • He was 13 years old when he started competing in kart races.
  • His career in Formula One started in 1984 at GP of Brazil with Toleman-Hart.
  • He was hired by Lotus-Renault in 1985.
  • In 1988, he was hired by McLaren-Honda, where his teammate, Alain Prost, became his greatest rival.
  • The first F1 World Championship he won was in 1988 (GP of Japan - Suzuka).
  • After an accident involving Alain Prost at GP of Japan in 1989, he was suspended from F1 for six months by Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA).
  • He won the F1 World Championship for the second time in 1990 (GP of Japan - Suzuka).
  • His first victory in Brazil was in 1991.
  • He became the youngest ever three-time F1 world champion in 1991.
  • He was hired by Williams-Renault in 1994.
  • He was 34 years old when he died in a race accident on the Tamburello's corner at GP of San Marino in May 1st, 1994.
  • Ayrton Senna is considered as one of the best F1 drivers of all times. His death served as a warning and motivated more investiments in competitors' safety, which continues until today.
